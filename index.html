  1. Select a Sample: Click on one of the 3 unknown rocks (A, B, or C).
  2. Weigh It: Click "WEIGH" to get the Mass (grams).
  3. Submerge It: Click "SUBMERGE" to find the Volume (mL) based on how much water it moves.
